Research

I'm interested in my area of research interest that lies at the intersection of Natural Language Processing(NLP), Computer Vision(CV), and Software Engineering leading to an infinite scope of creativity. I am fascinated about understanding the nuances of natural language and making machines understand the intent behind words. I believe that the most important investment in NLP would be to develop a paradigm that could learn the various target tasks with weak supervision without the need for a (large) labeled dataset. I want to work towards removing the gridlock which arises due to the unavailability or high costs associated with acquiring an annotated dataset.
